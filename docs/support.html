Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email. We'll send a link to reset it.

How do I change my email or password?

This isn't available in the app yet. Email us at budgetcuhapp@gmail.com from the address on your account and we'll help you update it.

How do I cancel or manage my BudgetCuh Pro subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through the App Store or Google Play, not directly by BudgetCuh. On iPhone: Settings > your name > Subscriptions. On Android: open the Play Store app > profile icon > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions. Find BudgetCuh and cancel or change your plan there.

Why isn't my receipt scanning correctly?

Make sure the receipt is well-lit and the full receipt is visible in frame. If a scan still looks wrong, you can always edit the transaction manually after it's added.

How do I delete my account and data?

Open the app, go to Profile > Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data right away and can't be undone. You can also request deletion by emailing us.

Where is my data stored, and who can see it?

See our Privacy Policy for details on what we collect, how it's used, and which third-party services we rely on.

Still stuck?

Email budgetcuhapp@gmail.com with a description of the issue and, if relevant, a screenshot — we read every message.
