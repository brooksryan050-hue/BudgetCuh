BudgetCuh Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

BudgetCuh ("we," "our," or "the app") is a personal budgeting app. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. BudgetCuh is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

Information We Collect

CategoryWhat it isLinked to your account?
Contact infoEmail address and display nameYes
Financial infoTransactions, budgets, accounts, balances, savings goals, currency, and monthly income you enterYes

We do not collect your location, contacts, browsing history, or any advertising identifier, and we do not use your data for tracking or advertising.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate core features: tracking transactions, budgets, savings goals, and challenges.
  • To generate AI coaching tips and receipt-scanning results, using your financial data and, if you use the scanner, your receipt photo. This is sent securely to our AI provider (Anthropic) to produce a response, and is not retained by them for training.
  • To manage your subscription and unlock premium features.
  • To send you a daily reminder or coaching notification, if you turn that on.

Third-Party Services

We share the minimum data necessary with the following providers to run the app:

  • Supabase, our backend database and authentication provider, which stores your account and financial data securely.
  • Anthropic, which powers the AI receipt scanner and coaching features. Your financial context and, when you use the scanner, your receipt photo are sent to Anthropic to generate a response.
  • RevenueCat, which manages subscriptions and links your purchase status to your account.
  • Apple, which processes any in-app purchases through the App Store and handles push notification delivery.

None of these providers may use your data for their own advertising purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase's cloud database and, for offline access, cached securely on your device. All traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). Access to your data is restricted to your own account through database-level security rules.

Your Rights

You can review and edit most of your information directly in the app (Profile tab). You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Profile > Delete account. This immediately and permanently removes your account, transactions, budgets, goals, and any other data tied to it. This action cannot be undone.

If you would prefer to request deletion by email instead, contact us at the address below.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is removed immediately, aside from anonymous, aggregated records that are not linked back to you.

Children's Privacy

BudgetCuh is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to budgetcuhapp@gmail.com.
